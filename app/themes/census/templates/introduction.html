The Census needs to include everyone.

This questionnaire contains two parts:

  • Household questions.
  • Individual questions.

The householder should complete the household questions.

The individual questions must be completed for every person who usually lives in this household. The householder can complete the survey on behalf of the individuals who usually live in their household or ask them to answer independently.

If any member of this household aged 16 or over wishes to complete their own questionnaire a separate form can be requested here.

Your personal information is protected by law and will be kept confidential for at least 100 years.
